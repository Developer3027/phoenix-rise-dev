Our Mission

The safety of humanity, and the preservation of dignity, compounded with the mission of hope, is paramount in rebounding from a loss suffered from natural disasters. Phoenix: Rise from The Ashes Foundation is that instrument of hope. To help others suffering, to know that there is a way to overcome insurmountable obstacles.

Testimonials

Glen Affonso

On a Saturday in February 2022, Stephanie visited, Faith Presbyterian Church in Indian Land, SC, seeking assistance for the victims of the Kentucky tornado. Motivated to offer immediate support, we collected essential items for three weeks and delivered them to Stephanie's office for transportation to Kentucky. Stephanie's presence has been a blessing in my life and the life of our church.

Craig Patterson

Stephanie reached out to me, after our state and three others were hit by an EF4 tornado on Dec 10, 2021. Stephanie needed suggestions, and information as she was single handedly bringing supplies for the victims. She drove 1000 miles round trip twice in one week to bring much needed help to Kentucky. I've never met anyone willing to do so much for people that she doesn't know. We have kept in touch, and I consider Steph a good friend now.
